Project Objective

A Complete Bus Booking
Management System

A full-stack web application built with Spring Boot and Thymeleaf for managing reservations, routes, schedules, and payments — all within a clean, intuitive interface.

Authentication & Security

JWT-based authentication with secure login, role-based access, and protected API endpoints.

Trip Search & Booking

Search trips by routes and timings, with seamless booking and trip management.

Seat Availability

Real-time seat tracking with interactive reservation and booking validation.

Payments & Bookings

Manage booking records, payment details, transactions, and customer history.

Agency & Fleet Management

Manage buses, drivers, and agency operations including scheduling and assignments.

Reviews & Analytics

Customer reviews, trip feedback, driver schedules, and performance insights.
